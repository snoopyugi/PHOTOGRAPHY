    Services We Provide

    Premium Photos

    Students learn to create a wide variety of photos. These photos offer more artistic, that have a higher production value, and a different, more unique aesthetics.
    Adobe Photoshop

    We use Adobe Photoshop to teach students how to edit their photos in class so they can eventually edit their own photos outside of class.
    Crello

    A visual editor that allows you to create social media images, add banners and posters, email headers, and other popular formats.
    Photo Printing

    We also teach students how to keep photos the old fashion way, which is to print the photos for them to keep.
